THE BAND

We love music

Glass Animals are a British indie rock band formed in Oxford in 2010. Led by singer, songwriter, and producer Dave Bayley, the group also features his childhood friends Joe Seaward, Ed Irwin-Singer and Drew MacFarlane. Bayley wrote and produced all three Glass Animals albums. Bayley spent his childhood in Massachusetts and Texas[3] before relocating to Oxford, where he met the other band members. The first, Zaba (2014), spawned the single "Gooey", which was eventually certified platinum in the United States.
